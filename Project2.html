INDIAN PREMIER LEAGUE




TEAMS

CHENNAI SUPER KINGS


Description :

-The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a highly successful Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise, based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It is owned by Chennai Super Kings Cricket Limited (CSKCL), which was formed after being demerged from India Cements. CSK has won the IPL title five times and is known for its consistent performance and strong fan following

ROYAL CHALLENGERS BENGALURU


Description :

-Royal Challengers Bangalore, commonly known as RCB, is an Indian professional franchise cricket team based in Bengaluru, Karnataka that competes in the Indian Premier League It is owned by United Spirits

MUMBAI INDIANS


Description :

-The Mumbai Indians are a professional Twenty20 cricket franchise based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. They compete in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and are owned by Reliance Industries. The team has been one of the most successful franchises in the IPL, winning the title five times.

KOLKATA KNIGHT RIDERS


Description :

-The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) is an Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise cricket team based in Kolkata, India. The team is co-owned by Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan (through Red Chillies Entertainment), actress Juhi Chawla, and her spouse Jay Mehta. KKR plays its home matches at the iconic Eden Gardens stadium.

DELHI CAPITALS


Description :

-The Delhi Capitals is a professional Twenty20 cricket team that represents Delhi in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2008 as the Delhi Daredevils, the team is co-owned by the GMR Group and the JSW Group

PUNJAB KINGS


Description :

-The Punjab Kings franchise unveiled a new logo and brand identity on February 17, 2021, transitioning from Kings XI Punjab. The new logo features a contemporary, evolved design that emphasizes the team's Punjabi heritage and the spirit of Punjab. It incorporates a lion motif, symbolizing exuberance, dynamism, and a distinctive edge. The color scheme is vibrant, reflecting the liveliness and vibrancy of the brand.

GUJARAT TITANS


Description :

-The Gujarat Titans are an Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. They were established in 2022 and play their home matches at the Narendra Modi Stadium. The franchise has recently undergone a change in majority ownership, with Torrent Group acquiring a 67% stake from CVC Capital Partners.

SUNRISERS HYDERABAD


Description :

-Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is an Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise based in Hyderabad, Telangana. The team was founded in 2012 after the Deccan Chargers, another Hyderabad-based team, was terminated. The team is owned by Kalanithi Maran of the SUN Group.

RAJASTHAN ROYALS


Description :

-The Rajasthan Royals (RR) is an Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise based in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Founded in 2008, it was one of the original eight IPL teams. The team is primarily owned by the Royals Sports Group
